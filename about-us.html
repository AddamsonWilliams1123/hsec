ABOUT US

Hell Security is a highly skilled and determined hacker group

founded by former members of the well-known hacking collective Kira Security.

With extensive expertise in cybersecurity, digital infiltration, and intelligence gathering,

we have evolved into a formidable force dedicated to making the world a safer and freer place.

OUR MISSION

At Hell Security, our primary objective is to rid the world of predators—those

who exploit and harm the most vulnerable members of society.

We relentlessly track down and expose pedophiles, zoophiles, and necrophiles, ensuring they face justice.

We also fight against:

    Government Corruption – Exposing abuses of power and fighting for human rights.

    Terrorism and Violence – Disrupting networks that spread chaos.

    Human Rights Violations – Standing for freedom and justice.

    Internet Censorship – Fighting for a free and open web.

COLLABORATION WITH ANONYMOUS

Hell Security frequently collaborates with Anonymous,

other hacking groups, and individual hacktivists.

Through joint cyber operations, we enhance our impact and hold wrongdoers accountable.

OUR #OPS

    #OpChildSafety – Tracking and exposing child predators.

    #OpRussia & #OpBelarus – Fighting oppressive regimes.

    #OpSriLanka – Supporting democracy and human rights.

    #OpLatvia – Targeting political entities supporting oppressive regimes.
